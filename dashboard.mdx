## Dashboard Home
<div id="dashboard" />

Your main hub for repository management and optimization tracking.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Repository Management" icon="code-branch">
    Add and configure the repositories you want Polarity to monitor and refine.
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Connect Repositories">
        Click the **Add Repos** button to select which repositories Polarity should monitor
      </Step>
      <Step title="Configure Settings">
        Set up refinement preferences and model selection for each repository
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start Refining">
        Begin optimizing your pull requests with AI
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Tip>
      Need help getting started? Check out our [Quick Start Guide](/quickstart) for step-by-step instructions.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Activity Overview" icon="chart-simple">
    **Heatmap Visualization**
    
    Track your optimization activity over time with a visual heatmap showing how Polarity's refinements vary day by day.
    
    **Recent Improvements**
    
    View the latest pull requests refined by Polarity AI, including optimization details and performance metrics.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Important Team Notes

<Warning>
  **Before making team changes, please note:**
  
  - **Leaving a team** will revert you to the free trial plan
  - **Removing a team member** will revert them to the free trial plan
  - **Deleting your team** will send you and your entire team to free trial with free plan restrictions
  - **Upgrading seats** will cancel your current subscription and start a new one (your team stays intact)
</Warning>
